## About / Story

Instituto Soares is a dual-specialty clinic in Agualva-Cacém run by the Soares family. Netto Soares handles chiropractic care and therapeutic massage, while Roberta Soares specializes in advanced aesthetics. The clinic is known for its warm, personalized approach — every procedure is explained in detail, and follow-up care is always provided. Roberta has been working in aesthetics for at least 5 years (clients since 2019 before the institute opened). The space is consistently described as clean, hygienic, calm, and welcoming. Multiple reviews mention feeling "renewed" and "lighter" after treatments.
